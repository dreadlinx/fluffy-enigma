Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can you respond to a service request?

For standard service requests, we typically respond within 1 business day and can schedule service within 24-48 hours. For emergency requests, we aim to be on-site within 2-3 hours in our primary service area.

Do you work on all truck brands or just Volvo?

While we specialize in Volvo trucks, our technicians are certified to work on all major brands including Mercedes, MAN, Scania, Iveco, Renault, and more. We have the diagnostic tools and expertise for a wide range of heavy vehicles.

What are your payment terms?

We accept all major credit cards, EFT payments, and cash. For fleet accounts, we offer 30-day net terms with approved credit. Estimates are provided before any work begins, and payment is due upon completion of services.

Do you offer warranties on your repairs?

Yes, we stand behind all our work. Most repairs come with a 6-month or 10,000 km warranty (whichever comes first). Engine and transmission rebuilds have a 12-month warranty. Warranty terms are provided in writing with your service documentation.

Can you service trucks at our company facility?

Absolutely! Our mobile service model is designed to come to your location. We can perform most repairs and maintenance at your facility, depot, or even roadside. Our fully-equipped service trucks have everything needed for comprehensive repairs.

How do I request a quote for service?

You can request a quote by calling us directly, using the contact form on this page, or emailing sammusarurwa239@gmail.com. Please provide as much detail as possible about your truck and the service needed for an accurate estimate.
