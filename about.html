Who We Are

Founded in 2008, Samtec Mobile Truck Clinic began as a small operation with a single service vehicle and a passion for truck mechanics. Today, we've grown into a trusted name in the heavy vehicle repair industry, specializing in Volvo trucks while serving all major brands.

Our founder, Samuel T. Johnson, started the company with a simple vision: to provide exceptional mobile repair services that minimize downtime for truck operators. What began as a one-man operation has evolved into a team of certified technicians serving clients across the region.

At Samtec, we believe that trucks are the backbone of commerce, and keeping them running is essential to our economy. That's why we've dedicated ourselves to delivering the highest quality repairs with the convenience of mobile service.

Our Journey

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted name in mobile truck repairs, our journey has been driven by a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction.

What started in a small garage has grown into a fleet of fully-equipped mobile workshops serving clients across the region. Throughout our growth, we've maintained the personal touch and attention to detail that characterized our earliest days.

Our milestones reflect not just business growth, but the trust we've built with hundreds of clients who rely on us to keep their trucks running smoothly.
